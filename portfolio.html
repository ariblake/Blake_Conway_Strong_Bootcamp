The start of Chico's Adventure.

Jen Dafoe, author and creator of the book series "Chico's Adventures", approached Matchstick to re-brand and create a website for Chico the dog to help kids conquer their fears of going to the hospital and the dentist. Thrilled to work on this project, the Matchstick team started brainstorming and striking flames. This is how the build turned out.

Chico gets branded.

We started this project by looking at the previous brand and website. Chico is a fun and creative dog, which needed to be the focus of the new design. We needed to create a website that is professional, but still fun and kid-friendly.

We chose a colour palette that was bold and eye-catching to appeal to children, while our fonts were simple and easy to read.

We created an entire new brand and style for Chico that impressed and wowed our new client.

Chico gets built.

As Hillary and Sarah finished up the design and layout, Ariana started to build the site.

The purpose of the new build was to make it easy to read with a nice flow. We achieved this with a one-page, simple block layout, sectioned off into different groups of information.

After 4 months of working on the project, we were finally finished. Ta-da! Enjoy Chico's Adventures' new prescence.
