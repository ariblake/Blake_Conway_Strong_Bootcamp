Spark Creativity

We love what we do and are great at doing it.

Matchstick is a full-service creative agency with a purpose-driven approach. The things we do are of real value to others. This stems from our values which include being talented, passionate and fiery. We start a fire to ensure our clients are setting the bar high in their industries, communities, and culture.

This is our story.

In 2016, three school classmates met and founded their own advertising and marketing agency. This agency has repeatedly shown growth and change over the years, better adapting to clients, technology and surroundings. Ariana, Sarah and Hillary all attend Western University and Fanshawe College for Interactive Media Design. Each founder has developed a passion for design, code and video and wanted to share their skills with members of the London, Ontario community.

The Matchstick team is a perfect match and will share their creativity every strike of the way with you.
